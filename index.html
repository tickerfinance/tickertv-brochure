Who are Copia?

A London digital media company built to support the financial calendar – and more.

Copia is a London-based digital media company. We are trusted to create and deliver live-streamed and pre-recorded video and audio events. These often take place at points of extreme pressure in the financial calendar for those involved, when quality support, rapid turnaround and accurate execution are essential.

We have a wide range of services that allow companies and investment firms from different sectors and sizes to communicate effectively online and reinforce their investment case. Some examples include video and audio interviews, company events and B2B/B2C corporate videos.

We are passionate about our work and enjoy working with companies directly or through their corporate advisors. Building strong relationships with all involved is key to the delivery of excellent digital content.

Our full-service offer

Everything your online IR needs, in one team.

Ticker TV is a full-service IR video and social media package. We work with leading listed businesses and Fund Management companies. Our experienced team has deep financial knowledge and serious technical expertise.

01 · Interviews

Ticker TV

We specialise in online CEO and senior management video interviews. These are recorded as-live on special software for higher definition, released on our Ticker TV YouTube channel and distributed widely on social media. We put an RNS release in context or help reinforce key strategic messages with authority.

02 · Strategic

Fireside Chat

Longer, less news-driven, more strategic interviews. Alongside an active X or LinkedIn account, these fill a lot of gaps in an annual comms strategy. Long-term shareholders find them particularly useful, as they invariably include lots of fresh information, and new investors find more than enough to Watchlist the company.

03 · Community

Town Hall Meetings

Live shareholder Q&As that build community and trust. When we work with AIM & ASX-dual-listed gold miner Greatland Resources, several hundred watch live and 6,000+ view the recording. 160 long-term shareholders attended the latest Town Hall Meeting from across the UK.

04 · Events

Event Coverage

Copia produce major annual financial and tech events such as the Investor Summit, GopherCon and RustNation. The quality of our event video content is second to none: superb room lighting and AV, every speaker and panel filmed and released afterwards, with compelling marketing videos and short interviews.
